Welcome!

Welcome to my professional resume and work portfolio website. You will find information here about my educational background, experience in teaching and instructional design, and a little bit about my path to higher education and instructional design as a career. In addition to educational and professional information, you will find out a little more about me, my strengths, and passion. You will also have an opportunity to review some of the projects that I created on my way to earning my Education Specialist degree in Learning Technologies and Design.

The goals and objectives of this website are to provide:

  • Information about my educational background
  • Information about my professional work experience and background
  • Interactivity to view projects created that demonstrate mastery of skills and concepts from the Educational Specialist program
  • Method of contacting me for more information

This site is a space where I have aggregated some of the projects from my courses in the Learning Technologies and Design program in completing the Educational Specialist degree. This site is intended to demonstrate some of the skills I have learned and projects that I have created during this time which also include educational projects that teach concepts and lessons in various areas.
